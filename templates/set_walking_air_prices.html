Air Cargo Pricing for Walk-In Customers

Air cargo uses special weight-based pricing for shipments over 3kg. Each weight tier has a per-kilogram rate.

Tiers: 3-10kg, 10-25kg, 25-50kg, 50-100kg, 100+kg. For shipments up to 3kg, standard bracket pricing applies.

Insurance Settings

Configure the percentage for insurance calculations based on declared value.

Global Insurance Percentage
%
This percentage will be applied to the Insured Amount (Declared Value) in order forms to calculate the Insurance Charge.
Calculation Example

If Insurance Percentage is set to 2%:

  • Insured Amount: ₹1,000
  • Insurance Charge: ₹20.00 (1,000 * 2 / 100)

The charge is automatically added to the total amount when "Insured Amount" is entered by staff.

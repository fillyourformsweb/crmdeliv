How Our CRM System Works
Walk-in Orders

Customers come directly and place orders. Prices are calculated based on destination state and weight. Quick processing with immediate receipt generation.

Client Orders

Corporate clients with pre-negotiated rates. Custom pricing can be set per state. Receives consolidated billing and account management.

Real-time Tracking

Every order gets a unique tracking number. Status updates throughout journey: pending → confirmed → in transit → delivered/RTO.

Billing Management

Automatic invoice generation. Payment tracking with received vs due amounts. Consolidated bills for corporate clients with GST support.

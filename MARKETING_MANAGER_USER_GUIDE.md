## How Marketing Managers Can Book Walk-in Orders

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

#### 1. **Login to the System**
   - Use your Marketing Manager credentials
   - You will only see walk-in order options

#### 2. **Navigate to New Walk-in Order**
   - Look for **"New Walk-in"** button in the main orders page
   - Or go to menu: **Orders → New Walk-in**

#### 3. **Fill Customer Information**
   - **Customer Name**: Full name of the sender
   - **Contact Number**: Valid phone number
   - **Email Address** (optional)
   - **Sender Pincode**: Exactly 6 digits
   - **Pickup Address**: Full address where package is collected from

#### 4. **Enter Receiver Information**
   - **Receiver Name**: Name of recipient
   - **Receiver Number**: Contact number
   - **Delivery Address**: Complete delivery address
   - **City & State**: Select from list
   - **Receiver Pincode**: 6 digits
   - **Landmark** (optional): Nearby location for reference

#### 5. **Select Shipping & Payment Mode**
   - **Shipping Mode**: Choose service type
     - Standard Delivery
     - Prime Express
     - Parcel Service
     - State Express
     - Road Express
     - Air
   
   - **Payment Mode**: How customer will pay
     - Cash Payment
     - UPI / Scanner
     - Online Transfer
     - Card Payment
     - Credit / To-Pay

#### 6. **⭐ SELECT PRICE LIST (Important!)**
   
   This is the **key feature** for Marketing Managers:
   
   Choose one of three pricing options:
   
   | Option | Description | Best For |
   |--------|-------------|----------|
   | **Standard Pricing** | Default pricing based on weight and location | Regular customers |
   | **Normal Client Price** | Structured client pricing | Corporate/bulk orders |
   | **Price Master** | Premium pricing with special rates | VIP/high-value orders |
   
   Your selection will:
   - Calculate the appropriate charges
   - Be saved with the order
   - Show up in all order views for reference

#### 7. **Package Details**
   - **Weight (kg)**: Package weight in kilograms
   - **Quantity (Boxes)**: Number of boxes
   - **Package Description**: What's in the package
   - **Special Instructions**: Any handling notes

#### 8. **Amount & Payment**
   - **Amount Received**: How much customer is paying
   - **Insured/Declared Value** (optional): Item value for insurance

#### 9. **Create Order**
   - Click **"Create Order"** button
   - System will generate a unique tracking number
   - Order will appear in your walk-in orders list

## How to View Your Orders

### Location 1: Main Orders List
- Go to **Orders → All Orders**
- Look for orders with **orange left border** (your bookings)
- See **"Marketing Manager"** badge next to receipt number
- Price list type shown in parentheses: "(Client Price)" or "(Price Master)"

### Location 2: Walk-in Orders List
- Go to **Operations → Walk-in Orders**
- Your orders marked with orange highlighting
- Easy to filter and track

### Location 3: Order Details
- Click any order to view full details
- See price list type in the **Billing Summary** section
- All customer and payment information visible

## Color Coding System

### How to Identify Your Orders:

**Visual Indicator**: Orange/Amber highlighting
```
┌─ Orange Border (4px)
│
│  Order #WALKIN001234
│  [Marketing Manager Badge]
│
│  Amount: ₹5,000 (Price Master)
└─ Orange Background
```

- **Light Orange Background**: Your booking
- **Orange Left Border**: Visual accent
- **"Marketing Manager" Badge**: Your role indicator
- **Price Type in Parentheses**: Which price list was used

## Important Notes

### Restrictions for Marketing Managers:
- ❌ Can **NOT** create client/corporate orders
- ✅ Can **ONLY** book walk-in (casual) customers
- ✅ Must select a price list for each order

### Price List Usage:
- **Default (Standard Pricing)**
  - Used when price strategy is not specified
  - Based on weight and destination state
  - Most common option

- **Normal Client Price**
  - For regular business customers
  - Structured pricing model
  - May include volume discounts

- **Price Master**
  - Premium pricing tier
  - Higher rates for special services
  - For priority/VIP customers

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if I select the wrong price list?
**A:** Contact your manager to edit the order. You can't change it yourself after creation.

### Q: Why do I only see walk-in order option?
**A:** Marketing Managers are restricted to walk-in bookings only. Regular staff and managers can create both types.

### Q: What's the difference between price lists?
**A:** 
- Standard: Best for common deliveries
- Normal Client: For structured corporate/B2B deals
- Price Master: Premium rates for priority service

### Q: Does the price list affect final billing?
**A:** Yes, the pricing structure used affects all calculations and the invoice generated.

### Q: Can I see orders created by others?
**A:** Yes, you can view all orders. Marketing Manager bookings are tagged with the badge for easy identification.

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Normal Client Price" option not showing
**Solution**: Verify you're logged in as a marketing manager. Only your role sees this option.

### Issue: Order not showing price list type
**Solution**: Ensure you selected a price list (not default) when creating the order.

### Issue: Can't access walk-in order form
**Solution**: Contact admin to verify your user role is set to 'marketing_manager'

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Always fill mandatory fields** (marked with *)
2. **Use exact 6-digit pincodes** for both sender and receiver
3. **Select appropriate price list** based on customer type
4. **Include special instructions** if package needs careful handling
5. **Verify receipt number** before submitting
6. **Check order confirmation** after creation
